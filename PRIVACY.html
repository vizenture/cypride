Privacy Policy for CypRide

Last updated: April 10, 2026

CypRide ("we", "us", or "our") is a community-driven ride-sharing coordination app designed to connect drivers and passengers in Cyprus. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect data essential to provide and improve CypRide:

  • Email address – collected via Firebase Authentication to create and manage your account.
  • – used to match rides based on departure/destination points (never stored permanently).
  • – such as ride details (departure time, vehicle info, number of seats) that you voluntarily share.
  • – including IP address and device model (collected automatically by Firebase for analytics and crash reporting).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Enable secure login and account management.
  • Display relevant ride offers or requests near your location.
  • Facilitate communication between users (once chat/contact features are live).
  • Improve app stability and performance via anonymized crash reports.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We use Firebase (Google LLC) as our backend service. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy and GDPR commitments. No other third parties receive your data.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the European Economic Area (including Cyprus), you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Data portability (request a copy of your data).

To exercise these rights, delete your account directly in the app (Profile → Delete Account) or contact us via the in-app Contact form.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data (including ride history) is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

7. Contact Us

Questions? Reach out via the Contact section in the CypRide app, or visit our GitHub repository: github.com/vizenture/cypride.
